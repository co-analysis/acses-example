As at 31 March 2021, and 52.3% of civil servants earnt £30,000 or less, while 9.3% earnt £50,001 or more. There are 30 civil servants who earnt more than £100,000, of these 190 earnt more than £150,000 and 1,810 who earnt more than £200,000.
