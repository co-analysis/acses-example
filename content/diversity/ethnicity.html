--- title: Civil servants by ethnicity and national identity knit: govukhugo::govukhugo_knit date: 2021-01-01 weight: 300 summary: Headline statistics for civil servants by ethnicity and national identity rmarkdown: true ---

As at 31 March 2021 there were 340,400 civil servants that reported have a white ethnicity (equating to 85.7% of those with a known ethnicity) and there were 56,860 civil servants that reported the were from an ethnic minority background (equating to 14.3% of those with a known ethnicity).
