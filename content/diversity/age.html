--- title: Civil servants by age date: 2021-01-01 weight: 200 summary: Headline statistics for civil servants by age rmarkdown: true ---

As at 31 March 2021, the median age of civil servants was 45 years. There are 78,750 civil servants aged under 30 (16% of those whose age is known) and 49,350 civil servants aged 60 or over (10% of those whose age is known).

The Civil Service's most junior responsibility level has the largest proportion of staff aged under 30, and the largest proportion of staff aged 60 or over. The Senior Civil Service Level has the largest proportion of staff aged 40 and 49, and the largest proportion of staff aged between 50 and 59.
