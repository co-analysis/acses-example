Civil servants by organisation

As at 31 March 2021, the Civil Service headcount1 stands at 484,880, an increase of 28,4702. On a full-time equivalent (FTE) basis3 Civil Service employment stands at 452,830.

The Civil Service Statistics reports on 106 organisations, which are further aggregated into 39 departmental groups4.
